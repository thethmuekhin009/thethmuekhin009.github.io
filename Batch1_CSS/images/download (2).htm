Superhero Names

Jennifer Moss
August 27, 2019
Comments closed

Episode #38 – Jennifer and Mallory discuss names of famous superheroes (and supervillains) from the comics, movies, and television. We also report on current celebrity baby news and answer letters from you, our listeners!

Transcript

Names mentioned in this episode:
Soroya, Alyssia, Buck, Clark, Bruce, Harley Quinn, Natalia, Diana, Ronan, Kraven, Nefaria, Callisto/Callista, Lillith, Buffy, Hiro…and more!

Our sources for this episode include:
Brittanica.com, Batman Fandom, Newsweek, Wikipedia, ComicBook.com, Marvel.com, and ComicVine
